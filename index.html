The Global Carbon Marketplace

For companies to purchase carbon credits and futures to offset their greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. 

For investors to easily access a new and rapidly growing asset class on a transparent, fast and secure online exchange.  

For suppliers of carbon credits to certify and sell carbon credits and futures and fund carbon credit generating projects.

THE PROBLEM 

SCIENCE

Within the next 2 decades, global temperatures are likely to rise 1.5 degrees Celsius.

The 7 warmest years in the 1880-2020 record have all occurred since 2014.

PEOPLE

800 million people are vulnerable to climate change impacts, such as droughts, floods, heat waves, extreme weather events and rising sea-levels (UN, 2021).

BIODIVERSITY

More than 1 million species are at risk of extinction by climate change (National Geographic, May 2019). 

THE OPPORTUNITY

CONSUMERS are demanding accountability 

34% consumers reported having ‘Chosen brands that have environmentally sustainable practices/ values’ (Deloitte, Mar 2021).

BUSINESSES’ demand for carbon Net-Zero solutions is exploding

6x companies joined The Climate Pledge for Net-Zero in 2021 (The Climate Pledge, Dec 2021)

GOVERNMENTS are enforcing climate change actions

The EU, US, China, India and other governments are implementing climate accountability legislation (European Commision 2021, The White House, January 2021, Reuters, October 2021, BBC, November 2021)
