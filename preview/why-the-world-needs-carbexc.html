Why the world needs CARBEXC?

Martin 31.12.2021

Here are a few reasons why we believe that the world needs a solution like CARBEXC.

The demand for carbon neutrality and Net-Zero solutions is exploding.

Within the last two years, companies with accumulated annual revenue of +$10T have pledged carbon Net-Zero by 2040 - e.g. Alibaba, Amazon, Microsoft, Reckitt, Infosys, Siemens, Unilever, Coca Cola, Heineken, IBM, IPG, Snap Inc, ASOS, Salesforce, P&G, Accenture, Palo Networks, WPP, Klarna, Deloitte, Mercedes, Visa, Pepsico, Canva, Verizon, Uber to name a few of the 217+.

Carbon credits will conservatively become a $160 billion market opportunity according to Marin Katusa, Founder of Katusa Research, Fund Manager, best-selling author and world-renowned speculator.

Governments are enforcing climate change actions.

The European Climate Law (July 2021) set a legally binding target of Net-Zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050. The EU Institutions and the Member States are bound to take the necessary measures at EU and national level to meet the target (European Commision 2021). The US is initiating similar steps (The White House, January 2021). China pledged to become carbon neutral before 2060 and boosted their pledge from 20% energy from renewables to 25% by 2030 (Reuters, October 2021).
