Carbon Exchange

A Carbon Credit Exchange solution with a digital wallet, carbon credit tokens and a marketplace webshop. 

Since day one our missions has been to use the power of Decentralized Finance (DEFI) to empower everyone to save our planet.

1 - Trustworthy

  • Tokenized carbon credits
  • Blockchain-based settlement
  • Non-custodial mobile wallet and client side bidding

2 - Easy and accessible

  • Fast credit card checkout
  • Instant settlement
  • Transferable & tradable digital token

3 - Quality and Impact

  • CARBEXC certified carbon credit forwards
  • All VCS-certified carbon credits
  • Carbon credit offset on demand

Exchange Platform

Our simple exchange interface allows anyone to easily understand and participate in the exchange of carbon credits and futures.

Digital Wallet

Store all your carbon credits or forwards on our non-custodial digital wallet.

Marketplace Webshop

Discover new carbon removal projects without having to understand anything about crypto or blockchain and check-out using Credit card, Apple Pay, SEPA or SWIFT.
