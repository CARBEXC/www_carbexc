Carbon Forwards

Carbon Forwards is a visionary new asset class for the Voluntary Carbon Markets (VCM), that enable companies to buy carbon credits of future carbon removal projects. Our focus on carbon removal projects is to increase the impact we have on slowing down climate change.

To create Carbon Forwards CARBEXC has developed partnerships and is developing a unique certification process that involves satellite imagery, drones and IoT devices to track the projects.

Providing ROI while funding carbon removal projects.

Buy, sell and trade carbon credit futures and help organizations making an impact towards the betterment of our planet and our species with high impact nature-based solutions.

Not just reducing carbon emissions. No - that’s cutting corners and isn’t enough. We catalyze carbon removal projects, to remove carbon out of the atmosphere for 2x impact.
