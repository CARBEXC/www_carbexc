Our Team

A TEAM WITH $500 MILLION IN PREVIOUS EXITS

Co-founder and CEO: Kristof De Spiegeleer

    • Kristof is a humanist & motivated entrepreneur, passionate about helping to make the world a better place. He has a history of inventing and developing new IT infrastructure systems (storage, automation, cloud computing). Kristof has founded more than 10 companies of which 6 have been acquired by large IT players. He was lucky to be part of the first teams building the Internet and has helped to grow datacenters & ISPs mainly in Europe. Currently Kristof is CEO of Incubaid, as well as the ThreeFold Foundation, and ThreeFold Tech. He strongly believes there is need for a decentralized, neutral, affordable, green internet (owned by billions).
  • Co-founder and COO: Martin Quach
    • Martin Quach is a visionary and stands for making happy homes. He is a serial entrepreneur, having co-founded four technology startups. He was nominated lead mentor for Techstars Portugal and has advised several technology startups from the bootstrapped iPaper to the venture backed SecretCityTrail to the NGO NuOceans. He hosts the podcast Masters of Grit where he interviews successful founders on their source. He lead growth for Red Bull TV and Lunar Bank have experience from Google advising top tier growth clients on how to create success online.
  • CTO: Oliver Lienhardt
    • Oliver Lienhardt is a business information scientist, blockchain and tokenization expert. He has more than 20 years of experience as IT architect and innovation manager. He, works and advise the blockchain/fintech industrie and was involved in various projects at IBM and SAP. He build software for digital business ecosystems and supply-chain track and trace systems and various other application stacks.
  • CFO: Fahad Qawasmi
